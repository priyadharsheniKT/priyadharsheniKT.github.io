Projects

Innovative Approaches to Wildfire Monitoring

Forest monitoring during wildfires is crucial especially in remote areas where accessibility is challenging. Traditional technologies such as satellite remote sensing and observation towers may leave uncertainties, particularly during the early stages of a fire. My project utilizes UAV-captured RGB and thermal (IR) imagery called the FLAME2 dataset to apply deep learning-based methodology for fast fire classification which include benchmark architectures such as Logistic Regression, LeNet, Vgg, MobileNet, ResNet, and a unique Flame network. Furthermore, I also developed a hybrid method by cascading the best deep learning-based classifier among all and localization system for frames identified as containing fire. To achieve precise fire localization, I employed the Maximally Stable Extremal Regions (MSER) method, complemented by Non-Maximum Suppression (NMS).

Graphical User Interface (GUI) in Java

I developed a Java-based Airline Reservation System using Swing for the GUI and JDBC for MySQL integration at Intellect Design Arena Limited (Chennai) for my project. Social Activities

In my public policy coursework at Sastra University, I contributed to a team project addressing the issue of improper disposal of sanitary napkins in Sennampatti village (Tanjore). Through comprehensive community engagement and research, we determined that incineration aligned best with government guidelines for safe disposal. We successfully implemented our solution by installing an electric incinerator in the village and conducted a targeted awareness campaign on menstrual hygiene. This initiative aimed not only to improve environmental health but also to empower local women with sustainable practices.
